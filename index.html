How to Play NBA Basketball Stars

NBA Basketball Stars is easy to learn but challenging to master, just like the real game. Here's how to get started:

  1. Select your preferred game mode (1v1 or 2v2)
  2. Choose your basketball player character inspired by NBA superstars
  3. Use the controls to move around the court
  4. Press the shoot button when you're in a good position
  5. Time your shots perfectly for better accuracy
  6. Use defensive moves to block opponents and steal the ball
  7. Score more points than your opponent to win the match

Practice makes perfect! The more you play, the better you'll become at timing your shots and executing defensive moves, just like real NBA stars.
